# Tour Show S1

## Goal
Deterministic demo show for tour seed 1. Provides a reproducible scenario for testing the tour coordinator, season progression, and competition scoring pipeline.

## Deliverables
- Seeded tour with deterministic corps assignments and schedule
- Performance scoring across multiple rounds
- Standings computation and final rankings

## Acceptance Criteria
- Tour runs to completion with seed 1 producing consistent results
- Standings reflect correct score aggregation
- All corps participate in scheduled competitions
