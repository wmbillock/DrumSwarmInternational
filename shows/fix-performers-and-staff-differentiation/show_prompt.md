## Objective
Reformulate the roles and tabs to clearly differentiate between staff and performers, ensuring that staff are recognized as trusted individuals with demonstrated expertise while performers are shown as unverified until auditioned or deemed good enough. Improve visibility and evolution mechanisms for performers.

## Deliverables
- Updated roles for performers and staff.
- Correct display of staff and performers in the talent system.
- Clear visual and design elements differentiating staff from performers.
- Enhanced functionality to show only performers in the "Performers" tab, excluding staff.

## Constraints
- Performers must be distinguished visually from staff.
- The "Performers" tab should only show performers, not staff.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Staff members are clearly marked as trusted individuals with demonstrated expertise.
- Performers are displayed separately and can join through audition or being good enough without an audition.
- The talent system accurately reflects the updated roles and displays them correctly.
