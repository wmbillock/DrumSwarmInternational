# Fix Performers and Staff Differentiation

## Decisions

- Define roles:
  - **Staff**: Trusted individuals with demonstrated expertise.
  - **Performers**: Individuals unverified until auditioned or deemed good enough.

## General Effect

Ensure that the distinction between staff and performers is clear to the audience. Staff should be visually distinct from performers, reflecting their trusted status.

## Constraints

- Performers are unverified until auditioned or deemed good enough.
- Performers can join with or without an audition.
- Performers should evolve over time but currently lack this functionality.
- The "Performers" tab in talent should only show performers and not include staff.

## Deliverables

- Clear differentiation between staff and performers.
- Updated roles for performers and staff.
- Correct display of staff and performers in the talent system.

## Swarm Prompt
### Objective
Reformulate the roles and tabs to clearly differentiate between staff and performers, ensuring that staff are recognized as trusted individuals with demonstrated expertise while performers are shown as unverified until auditioned or deemed good enough. Improve visibility and evolution mechanisms for performers.

- Correct display of staff and performers in the talent system.

- Performers must be distinguished visually from staff.
