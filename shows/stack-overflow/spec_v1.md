## Show Concept

**Stack Overflow** is a three-movement narrative that visualizes the debugging process as a live performance crisis and resolution. The corps discovers a critical production bug during performance, triggering an emotional and physical journey from panic to methodical investigation to triumphant deployment.

**Movement Structure:**
- **Movement 1 — Discovery:** Panic and chaos; alarms trigger disorientation
- **Movement 2 — Root Cause Analysis:** Methodical detective work; systematic order emerges
- **Movement 3 — Hotfix Deployment:** Triumphant release; cathartic resolution

---

## Musical Design

**Movement 1 (Discovery):** Alarm-driven soundscape emphasizing sonic panic and urgency. High-pitched warning tones, glitchy electronics, and dissonant orchestration create immediate tension and chaos.

**Movement 2 (Root Cause Analysis):** Transition to structured, methodical underscore. Music becomes more deliberate and investigative—sparse instrumentation building logical patterns that mirror problem-solving.

**Movement 3 (Hotfix Deployment):** Triumphant, cathartic resolution. Full orchestral swell with ascending melodic lines and percussive celebration of success.

## Visual Design

**Color Motif:** Red/green transitions representing failing tests → passing tests across all three movements.

**Formation Arc:**
- **Movement 1:** Staggered diagonal lines (chaos, disorientation)
- **Movement 2:** Tightening into grid patterns (order, structure, methodical thinking)
- **Movement 3:** Radiating outward (release, triumph, success cascade)

**Red-to-Green Phase Shifts:** Each movement boundary marks a clear color transition reinforcing the narrative and test-status metaphor.
