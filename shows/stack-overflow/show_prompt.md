## Objective

Build "Stack Overflow" — a three-movement show about debugging under pressure. Transform the concept of discovering and fixing a critical production bug into an executable DCI show design with drill, music, guard, and visual elements.

## Deliverables

1. Drill formations for all three movements (Discovery: diagonal chaos, Root Cause Analysis: grid patterns, Hotfix Deployment: radiant formations)
2. Music composition notes with movement-specific character (alarm soundscape, investigative underscore, triumphant resolution)
3. Guard choreography integrating red/green color motifs
4. Costume/uniform spec with red-green test-status integration
5. Lighting cue sheet for phase transitions
6. Complete show runsheet with timing

## Constraints

- Red/green color motif must remain consistent and readable from all audience angles
- Three distinct movements with clear narrative progression
- All designs executable by a standard DCI-level corps
- Formation transitions must support chaos-to-order-to-release arc

## Acceptance Criteria

- Formations are drillable and executable
- Color transitions are visible and impactful
- Music and drill transitions align precisely
- Concept is readable to audience without program notes
