# Show Prompt: chat-test

## Design Summary (<=30 words)
Signal Fire is a brass-driven call-and-response show where ember visuals and snapping guard phrases trigger surging brass hits, building into a unified full-field blaze.

## Concept
A relentless call-and-response between signalers and echoing forces. Precision pulses expand into a full-field blaze of sound and motion.

## Musical Direction
- Brass locked: trumpet + trombone in forte with punchy rhythm.
- Battery drives accents; front ensemble adds metallic textures and sustained pads.
- Woodwinds/effects minimal, reserved for transitions and shimmer.

## Visual Direction
- Palette: black base, ember red, signal gold accents.
- Staging: concentric arcs opening into diagonal spear lines; repeated flare pivots.
- Props: lightweight signal pylons used as alignment anchors and portals.

## Guard Direction
- Vocabulary: snap-and-throw flag phrases, angular weapon exchanges, low-to-high lift arcs.
- Formations: guard anchors arcs, then splits into counter-rotating wedges to amplify the signal/echo motif.

## General Effect
Every visual flare triggers a brass surge, escalating from precision to spectacle and ending in a unified blaze that feels inevitable and earned.

## Corps Implementation Checklist
- Lock brass voicing and phrase-level accents.
- Build battery ostinati that mirror brass punches.
- Stage arc-to-spear transitions at three major impact points.
- Train guard on snap timing to align with brass hits.
- Integrate signal pylons for set anchors and visual punctuation.

## Deliverables
- Updated brief artifact (concept, music, visual, guard, GE)
- Actionable rehearsal plan aligned to phrase-level impacts
- Finalized prompt ready for activation
