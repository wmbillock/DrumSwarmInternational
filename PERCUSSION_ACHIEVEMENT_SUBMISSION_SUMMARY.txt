HOW ACHIEVEMENTS WORK
================================================================================

EARNING ACHIEVEMENTS:

1. Performer executes a show/rehearsal
2. Performance data captured (timing, accuracy, dynamics)
3. Post-performance analysis runs automatically
4. Criteria checked against recorded metrics
5. Achievement(s) awarded if thresholds met
6. Points added to performer record
7. Trust score updated with weighted delta
8. Achievement visible on performer profile

EXAMPLE: "Precision Surgeon"
  Trigger: Snare drummer performs 20+ bar roll
  Measurement: Analyze timing variance per beat
  Criteria: All beats within ±5ms of calculated beat
  Result: If criteria met, award 5 points (uncommon tier)
  Trust Impact: 5 × 0.5 × 0.15 = 0.375 trust gain
  Multiple Awards: Can be earned multiple times per season

EXAMPLE: "The Clockwork" (Legendary)
  Trigger: Full 12+ minute show performance
  Measurement: Timing variance across entire show
  Criteria: All notes ±20ms from beat grid, sustained
  Result: If criteria met, award 13 points (rare tier)
  Trust Impact: 13 × 0.5 × 0.35 = 2.275 trust gain
  Cap: Only one legendary per season per performer

SEASON PROGRESSION:

Season 1 (Foundation):
  - 5-8 common achievements (1-2 pts each)
  - Total: ~8 points
  - Trust: 50.0 → 54.2

Season 2 (Mastery):
  - 5 uncommon achievements (4-8 pts each)
  - Total: ~27 points
  - Trust: 54.2 → 61.8

Season 3+ (Excellence):
  - 3-4 rare achievements (9-15 pts)
  - 0-1 legendary achievements (16-25 pts)
  - Total: ~55 points (season cap)
  - Trust: 61.8 → 78.5+
